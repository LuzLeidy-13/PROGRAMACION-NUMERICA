Introducción al curso

Numerical Programming is a branch of applied mathematics and computing that uses algorithmic methods to solve mathematical problems in an approximate but efficient way. Its importance lies in the fact that it allows addressing situations where exact solutions are impossible or too costly to compute, being applied in engineering, applied sciences, economics, and more.
